Our Story

Free Fire is a battle royale game developed and published by Garena. Since its launch in 2017, Free Fire has become one of the most popular mobile games worldwide, breaking records with over 500 million downloads globally.

The game has revolutionized the mobile gaming industry by bringing console-quality battle royale action to smartphones, making it accessible to players everywhere.

Weapon Tips

Assault Rifles

Best for medium-range combat. Control recoil by firing in short bursts.

SMGs

Perfect for close-quarters combat. High fire rate but lower accuracy.

Sniper Rifles

Ideal for long-range engagements. Find a good vantage point.
